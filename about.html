About Us

Random IT Solutions Pvt. Ltd. Is a totally IT solutions and services provider, RIS offering a diverse range of IT products (hardware and software) solutions and services. The company is leading that winning combination with unparalleled experience in IT consultancy, IT support, networking, hardware supply, repairing, maintenance and installations.

In addition to the above, it also has a specialized team which is always ready to score big with out-of-the-box ideas. The focused approach of the company’s management and people makes it possible to exceed the demanding expectations of customers, attain excellence in product development and maintain the preferred supplier status with most valuable clients. All the components manufactured in the company’s plant are made by highly qualified and skilled people having years of experience in their respective fields. We are headquartered at Gurugram.
